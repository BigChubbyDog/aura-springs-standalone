## Service Type Multipliers

Applied to the base calculated price:

- **Standard Cleaning**: 1.0x (no change)
- **Deep Cleaning**: 1.5x
- **Move In/Out**: 1.8x
- **Airbnb Turnover**: 0.9x (discount for volume)
- **Post Construction**: 2.5x
